MangaMotion AI
Open-Source AI Platform for Transforming Manhwa into Animated Videos
________________________________________
Vision
MangaMotion AI is an open-source application that transforms manhwa or manga chapters (used only when the user has the necessary rights or permission) into short animated videos using only free and open-source AI models.
The goal is to automate as much of the animation pipeline as possible while still allowing the user to edit and improve the generated result.
The entire project should be runnable locally on a consumer GPU (RTX 3060 12GB or better).
________________________________________
Objectives
•	Upload a complete chapter
•	Detect individual pages
•	Detect every panel automatically
•	Read dialogue
•	Understand the story
•	Generate cinematic prompts
•	Animate each panel
•	Generate voices
•	Generate sound effects
•	Generate background music
•	Merge everything into a downloadable MP4
•	Allow manual editing before export

Prompt Generation
Instead of using the original panel only, the AI generates a cinematic prompt.
Example:

Camera performs a slow dolly-in.
